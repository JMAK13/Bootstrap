Syrus is a team of S.T.E.M. students from the Riverside and Norco areas of California. This year we have been selected to pariticpate in the N.A.S.A. SUITS design challenge to create an augmented reality interface for upcoming astronaught suits utilizing the Microsoft Hololens.

The Microsoft Hololens is a game-changing technology that crosses the boundaries of Virtual Reality into Augmented Reality. We use Unity3D to create the applications for the Hololens where we then progress to rigorous testing.
