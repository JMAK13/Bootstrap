The Tech

We are using several different devices in creating our interface. Mainly, we have the Microsoft Hololens to create the Augmented Reality environment in which we are working. We also have an Intel Compute Stick mounted to the astronaught's waist and a Leap Motion Sensor that clips onto the front-top of the Hololens. The Intel Compute Stick and Leap Motion Sensor allow for us to create more precise and custom gestures for the Hololens that make it easier for astronaughts to navigate through the program interface we designed.

We decided to go with this approach of using Leap Motion technology because it would definitely make us stand out from other teams. We also wanted astronaughts to be able to have intuitive gestures in order to be able to navigate through the system we designed making their tasks much easier and less dangerous.

The Goal

The goal of the Syrus team is to provide the best augmented reality interface that makes astronaught's jobs more safe and less difficult. We want to have a communication system that allows for astronaughts to communicate with headquarters, telemetry data responses that warn astronaughts about how much oxygen they have left, and intuitive gestures for our interface that allow astronaughts to easily navigate the UI we designed.

We also worked on an augmented reality navigation system that tells astronaughts where their next objective is located, and efficiently displayed task messages that astronaughts can toggle on and off when they need to see what their tasks require them to do.

The NASA S.U.I.T.S. Competition was designed to bring together ideas, concepts, projects, hardware, software, e.t.c. in order to effectively provide NASA with enough information, research, and work in order to include some of these in the design for upcoming astronught suits.

The Team

The Syrus team is based in Riverside and is made up of students from the Riverside and Norco City Colleges. Some of us are studying to be programmers, engineers in various fields such as electrical and mechanical, and designers. Some of our team members have already participated in this competition in 2017/18; however, we do have some new people joining us this year.
